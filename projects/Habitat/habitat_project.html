  • Habitat for Humanity Individual Project

We spent five weeks designing a house for Habitat for Humanity on a site in South Bend, IN. There were no explicit requirements, but we worked with the organization to hear what homeowners usually desired, and our main goal was to design a house that would eliminate the stigmas surrounding social housing.
