  • Masonry Pizza Oven

Throughout the summers of 2023 and 2024, I embarked in a personal project to design and build a masonry pizza oven. I was inspired to work on a masonry project after learning about its characteristics and techniques in my building technology class, but wasn't able to work with it hands-on due to the pandemic. Additonally, we had just removed a 100 year old brick chimney from family's cottage, and so i was motivated to use this reclaimed brick for another purpose. After plenty of research on the best designs of a pizza oven and how to ensure functionality, I was able to begin construction with the assistance of my aunt. I poured the concrete slab foundation in the summer of 2024 and created the CMU base. The following summer, I poured the concrete slab to support the cooking surface, built up a perimeter of chimney brick and laid the fire brick inside. Finally, after careful measuring and cutting of the bricks, I assembled the barrel vault with a fire brick interior, insulative blanket, chimney brick exterior, and a common brick facade.
