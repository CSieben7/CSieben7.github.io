  • Habitat for Humanity Group Project

A group project project for the St. Joseph County chapter of Habitat for Humanity. Based on our individual designs and their similarities, we were assigned into groups to work together and design an entirely original house, combining the best of each of our ideas. Our final product was a marketing sheet and a full set of construction drawings. The houses from this studio are now in the catalog of the St. Joseph County Habitat for Humanity for future homeowners to choose from. Additional credit on this project goes to group members Rob Baranko and Frances Harrington.
