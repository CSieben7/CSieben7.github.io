  • Golden Sun Youth Center: A New Mental Health Facility

For my senior thesis project, I decided to design a mental health center in my hometown of Palatine, Illinois. The goal of this building is to be the hub of mental health care in the Northwest suburbs of Chicago. With a focus on fostering community and connectivity, this center utilizes therapeutic design and psychological principles to do so. It provides spaces for various forms of therapy, socializing, coworking, and overnight stays. This structure is meant to be a safe space for kids of all different walks of life. As a supplemental part of our designs, we were also required to complete a technical packet about the structure, HVAC, and code compliance of the building.
