The first half of the semester was spent developing a group master plan for the area known as Yamacraw Village in Savannah, GA. The latter half was for our individual design project within the master plan and further refining the urban plan in a chosen block, as well as a comparitive plate of our building type. I chose to design mixed use housing, with a bookshop cafe on the ground floor and apartment units above.
