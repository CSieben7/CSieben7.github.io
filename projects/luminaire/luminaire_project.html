  • Painting with Light Class

This was an elective class that focused entirely on the history, design, construction, and restoration of stained glass. Along with learning, we were able to have hands-on experience with cutting and painting glass, with a midterm project of designing and painting a luminaire proposal. Our second half of the semester was involved with our final project of an assessment of the stained glass at the St. Adalbert's Catholic Church, with a full report of any damage to the accessible windows.
