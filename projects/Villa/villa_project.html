  • Villa Project

The goal of this project was to understand the Italian Villa typology, with its sequence from formal to informal gardens. Our assigned program for the villa was a homeless shelter, and the site was the Caelian Hill next to the Colosseum in Rome. We each created a masterplan for the site and designed a villa to house the given program.
