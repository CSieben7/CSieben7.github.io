  • Residential Addition

This is a house addition project that I was assigned at my internship at McNicholas Architects. Originally it was an old barn that the owners had converted into residential space, but they wanted to start a family and expand the place. As the sole designer, I reported to the principal of the firm to meet the client's desires for a partial demolition with an extension to the existing structure. In AutoCAD I completed a demolition plan as well as proposed floor plans and elevations, with multiple iterations of the sculptural brick feature the client requested.
